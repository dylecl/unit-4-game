CRYSTAL COLLECTOR

THE RULES

  • You will be given a random number at the start of the game.
  • There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amout of points to your total score.
  • You win the game by matching your total score to the random number. You lose the game if your score goes above the random number.
  • If your scores matches the target you can press any gem to collect your win.
  • The value of the crystals are hidden until you click on each crystal.
  • Every round the crystal value changes!
